Scribbler too

How to use

  • Draw with your finger, Apple Pencil, or mouse.
  • Scribble adds jittered strokes and shape stamps.
  • Mirror/Kaleido reflect strokes in real time.
  • Two-finger tap = Undo. Three-finger tap = Redo (iPadOS).
  • Use Save to download a PNG of your art.

Tip: Add to Home Screen for a full-screen app.
